FAQs

How much spiral ham per person?
Plan about 0.5 lb cooked per adult-equivalent. With ~65% edible yield on bone-in spiral, that’s about 0.75 lb raw per adult-equivalent (boneless closer to ~0.55–0.6 lb raw).
Bone-in vs boneless?
Bone-in has more waste but great presentation; boneless yields more edible meat per pound. Choose the style so the yield matches what you’re buying.
Buffet vs plated?
For buffets with lots of sides, reduce appetite or event length to scale portions slightly.
Should I round up?
Yes—yields and carving losses vary by brand and cut. Rounding up avoids shortfalls.
Verify label weight (bone-in/bone-out) and serving plan with your market.
