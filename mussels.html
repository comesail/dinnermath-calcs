FAQs

How much mussels per person?
As a main dish, plan about 1.5 lb in-shell per adult. For a mixed buffet, about 1.1–1.2 lb per adult. Appetizers are about 0.75 lb per adult. Teens trend between adults and children; children usually eat about 60% of an adult portion.
Does this include shell weight?
Yes — amounts are for in-shell mussels. The per-person baselines already account for shell weight.
How many bags should I buy?
Choose a Bag size preset (Small 1 lb, Standard 2 lb, Party 5 lb) or pick Custom to enter your own weight. We convert total raw pounds into an about bag count and round up.
Why include leftovers and appetite?
Appetite (Light/Typical/Hearty), event length, and leftovers (+10% or +20%) let you tune the plan so you don’t run short.
How does pricing work?
Subtotal is rounded raw pounds × price per lb. Then we add your tax and any fees for an estimated total and per-person cost.

Quick notes

  • Assumptions (in-shell): Adults 1.5 lb; Teens 1.2 lb; Children 0.9 lb.
  • Course multipliers: Main 1.00; Mixed 0.75; Appetizer 0.50.
  • Context multipliers: Appetite (0.90 / 1.00 / 1.20); Event length (0.95 / 1.00 / 1.08 / 1.15); Leftovers (No / +10% / +20%).
  • Bag size presets: Small ≈ 1 lb; Standard ≈ 2 lb; Party ≈ 5 lb (or use Custom).
  • Bag count: About bags = rounded raw pounds ÷ bag size (rounded up).
  • Rounding: Raw purchase rounded to the nearest quarter-pound for easy shopping; bags rounded up.
  • Disclaimer: Estimates only; confirm with your seafood market and adjust for your crowd.
