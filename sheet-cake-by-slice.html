FAQs

What size is a full / half / quarter sheet?
Bakeries vary, but a common guide is Full ≈ 18″×24″, Half ≈ 13″×18″, Quarter ≈ 9″×13″. Yields above are based on typical cuts.
How do you choose slice size?
Party slices are smaller for big groups; generous slices suit intimate events. Pick the option that fits your crowd.
Should I add cupcakes?
If you’re short by 6–12 slices, cupcakes are an easy top-off instead of another whole pan.
