FAQs

How many steaks per person?
Plan about one steak per adult-equivalent for a plated main. Use the dropdown to scale up/down for buffet or hungry guests.
How do steaks convert to pounds?
We multiply the number of steaks by the raw ounces per steak for your chosen cut and convert to total pounds.
Which cut yields the most?
Sirloin tends to have slightly higher cooked yield than ribeye; New York strip is in between. The calculator accounts for these differences.
Round up or down?
Round up—thickness and trimming vary by butcher.
