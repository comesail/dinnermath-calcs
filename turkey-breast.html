FAQs

How much turkey breast per person?
Main entree baseline is about 6 oz cooked per adult, 7 oz per teen, and 4 oz per child. Thanksgiving buffet baseline is about 5 oz per adult, 6 oz per teen, and 3.5 oz per child. Sandwich buffet baseline is about 4 oz per adult, 5 oz per teen, and 3 oz per child. Tasting is about 2.5 to 3 oz per adult. Adjust with Course mode, Appetite, Event length, and Leftovers.
Boneless vs bone-in?
Boneless yields about 70 percent cooked meat from raw weight. Bone-in includes bones and generally yields about 55 to 60 percent. The calculator converts cooked needs to raw pounds based on your selection.
How is price calculated?
Subtotal equals rounded raw pounds times your price per lb raw. Then we add sales tax and any fees to estimate a total and cost per person.
Do you round up?
Yes, we round up raw pounds and show results as about. Use +10 percent or +20 percent leftovers for a buffer.
Any tips for thawing and cooking?
As a planning note, many grocers suggest refrigerating about 24 hours per 4 to 5 lb to thaw. Cook to a safe internal temperature per your local guidance.

Quick notes

  • Baseline per person cooked: Entree adults 6 oz, teens 7 oz, children 4 oz. Sandwich adults 4 oz, teens 5 oz, children 3 oz. Tasting adults 2.5 to 3 oz.
  • Yields: Boneless about 0.70, Bone-in about 0.58. Raw pounds = cooked pounds divided by yield.
  • Multipliers: Event length 0.95, 1.00, 1.08, 1.15. Appetite 0.90, 1.00, 1.20. Leftovers No, +10 percent, +20 percent.
