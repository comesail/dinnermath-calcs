FAQs

How much salad per person?
As a side, plan about 1.5 cups (about 2.5 oz) per adult, about 1.75 cups (about 3.0 oz) for teens, and about 1 cup (about 1.5 oz) for children. Entree salads are heavier; tasting flights are lighter. Appetite, event length, and leftovers adjust the estimate.
Garden vs Caesar — what changes?
We apply a small style tweak: Caesar runs about 1.10x the base (denser toppings), while Garden uses the baseline.
What do the bag sizes mean?
Common retail sizes are 5 oz clamshells, 10–12 oz bags, 16 oz (1 lb) bags, and 32 oz (2 lb) foodservice packs. We convert total ounces into about how many bags based on your selection.
How does pricing work here?
Subtotal = unit price applied to the rounded quantity for your mode: per bag (rounded bags x price per bag) or per pound (rounded pounds x price per pound). Then tax and any extra fees are added for a total and per-person estimate.
Do you round up?
Bags round up to whole bags; pounds round to the nearest 0.1 lb (display). Add +10% or +20% leftovers if you want extra buffer.

Quick notes

  • Base per-person (side): Adults about 2.5 oz; Teens about 3.0 oz; Children about 1.5 oz.
  • Course multipliers: Side 1.00x; Entree 1.60x; Party 1.20x; Tasting 0.70x.
  • Style multiplier: Garden 1.00x; Caesar 1.10x.
  • Leftovers options: No; +10%; +20%.
  • Disclaimer: Estimates only; confirm with your grocer/caterer and adjust for your crowd.
