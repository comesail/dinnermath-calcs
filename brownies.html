FAQs

How many brownies per person?
For mixed buffets, plan about 1.5 squares per adult (about 1.2 for teens and 0.9 for children). Appetizers/snacks are about 1 square per adult. Use appetite, event length, and leftovers to tune.
What’s the difference between homemade and store-bought?
We convert pieces into pans (homemade) or packs (store-bought) using your yield inputs, then round up to whole units for easy shopping.
How do serving styles change the amount?
Styles set per-adult baselines: Squares ≈ 1.5; Mini bites ≈ 3; Large ≈ 1. Teens ≈ 0.8 adult; Children ≈ 0.6 adult.
How much for 10, 25, 50, or 100 people?
For squares at typical appetite: 10 ≈ 15–18 pieces, 25 ≈ 38–45, 50 ≈ 75–90, 100 ≈ 150–180. Use the calculator for your exact mix and settings.
How is pricing calculated?
Subtotal is batches to buy × price per pan/pack. Then we add your tax and any fees to estimate a total and per-person cost.

Quick notes

  • Per-adult baselines (pieces): Squares ≈ 1.5; Mini bites ≈ 3; Large ≈ 1. Teens ≈ 0.8 adult; Children ≈ 0.6 adult.

  • Context multipliers: Appetite (0.90 / 1.00 / 1.20); Event length (0.95 / 1.00 / 1.08 / 1.15); Leftovers (No / +10% / +20%).
