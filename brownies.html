Quick Notes

  • Baseline: ~1 standard square per adult-equivalent.
  • Generous pieces: Use 1.3 per AE; for small party bites use 0.8 per AE.
  • Pans: Typical yields—8×8 ≈ 16, 9×9 ≈ 20, 9×13 ≈ 24 squares (depends on cut & thickness).
  • Round up: Brownies store/freeze well—extra squares are easy to keep.

FAQs

How many brownies per person?
Plan about 1 standard square per adult-equivalent. Use the size dropdown to go generous or smaller for big dessert spreads.
How many pans do I need?
We divide your total squares by the selected pan’s typical yield (8×8 ≈ 16, 9×9 ≈ 20, 9×13 ≈ 24) and round up.
What if brownies are very rich?
Pick “Small party squares” to reduce per-person needs, or set appetite to Light.
