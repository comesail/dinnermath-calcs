FAQs

How much crudite and dip per person?
As an appetizer, plan about 5 oz raw vegetables per adult or teen and about 3 oz per child. Dip baseline is about 1.5 oz per adult or teen and about 1.0 oz per child. Use Course mode, Appetite, Event length, and Leftovers to adjust. Grazing tables often need a little more.
Homemade vs jar or canned dip?
Homemade shows cups to prepare and can price per cup. Jar or canned shows cups and also converts to common jar sizes so you can buy whole containers and price per jar.
What jar sizes are supported?
Options include 8 oz, 12 oz, 15 oz, 16 oz, 24 oz, and 32 oz. The calculator rounds up to whole jars.
How are cups and pounds converted?
Dip uses fluid ounces to cups at 8 fl oz per cup. Vegetables are planned by raw weight. For convenience, we assume about 0.80 cooked yield if you blanch some items, but this tool outputs raw pounds to buy.
How is price calculated?
Vegetables subtotal equals rounded raw pounds times unit price per pound. Dip subtotal equals rounded cups times price per cup for Homemade or rounded jars times price per jar for Jar or canned. Estimated total adds sales tax and any fees, and cost per person divides by total guests.

Quick notes

  • Baseline per person: Adults and Teens about 5 oz raw vegetables and 1.5 oz dip. Children about 3 oz raw vegetables and 1.0 oz dip.
  • Course multipliers: Appetizer 1.00x, Grazing table 1.15x, Side with meal 0.85x, Tasting plate 0.70x.
  • Leftovers: No, +10 percent, or +20 percent applies at the end.
  • Jar sizes: 8, 12, 15, 16, 24, and 32 oz. Rounds up to whole jars.
  • Disclaimer: Estimates only; confirm with your grocer or caterer and adjust for your crowd.
