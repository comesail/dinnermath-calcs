FAQs

How many cheesecake slices per person?
Main dessert about 1 slice per adult; buffet about ¾ slice; minis about ½ slice. Teens ≈ 0.8 adult; children ≈ 0.6 adult.
How many slices in a cheesecake?
A standard 9" cheesecake has about 12 slices. Mini 4" cakes ≈ 4 slices. A sheet cheesecake can be cut into ≈ 48 slices.
What if I want leftovers?
Select +10% or +20% in leftovers. It increases slice and cake estimates.
How does pricing work?
We multiply cakes × your unit price, then add tax and fees for total cost.
Does this include toppings or sides?
No—just cheesecake. Add extras separately.
