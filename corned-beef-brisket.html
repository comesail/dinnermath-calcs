Mini FAQ

How much raw brisket per person?
For a main dish, plan about ¾ lb raw per adult (teens ~½–¾, kids ~½ of adult). This accounts for shrinkage so cooked portions land around ½ lb.
Flat vs point — which to pick?
Flat is leaner and slices evenly; point has more fat and flavor but cooks down more. The calculator adjusts shrinkage accordingly.
Are results raw or cooked?
Raw pounds to buy are the headline; the cooked yield is shown in the pills for serving planning.
