FAQs

How many whole chickens per person?
For a main course at typical appetite, plan about 1 whole 4.0-pound chicken per 3 adult-equivalent guests. Use the controls to scale for appetite, event length, mixed menus, and leftovers.
Do I enter raw or cooked weight?
Select the raw weight per whole chicken (3.5, 4.0, 4.5 pounds). The calculator estimates servings and cooked yield for you.
What if I am carving ahead or serving family-style?
The servings model already assumes typical buffet or family-style service. If plates are very generous, increase appetite or leftovers.
Can I mix whole chickens with other mains?
Yes. Choose Mixed menu so chicken takes a smaller share, or reduce appetite to “Light” if guests will sample many dishes.
How is cooked yield handled?
Whole chickens include bone and skin. We use ~58% cooked yield from raw weight for estimating cooked meat; this is already baked into the servings math.
