FAQs

How many pounds of snow crab legs per person?
For a main, plan roughly 8–12 ounces per adult (about ½–¾ lb raw). For appetizers, plan 3–5 ounces per adult. Adjust for appetite, event length, and leftovers.
What does “clusters per pound” mean?
It’s a size grade—how many clusters make one pound. We use it to estimate total clusters and per-person averages.
Are results raw or cooked?
Raw purchase weight. Cooked yield varies with prep.
