FAQs

How much Dungeness crab per person?
Plan about 2 lb per adult (whole, in shell) for a main course. Teens count as about three-quarters of an adult and children about half. Use appetite and leftovers to fine-tune.
How many whole crabs is that?
Average whole Dungeness crabs weigh about 2 lb each. Edit the average weight field to convert pounds to an estimated crab count that matches your supplier.
What if I’m serving picked crab meat?
This tool sizes whole crabs in shell. For meat only, assume about 20–25% edible yield from whole crab and increase the raw pounds accordingly.
How do leftovers work?
Choose +10% or +20% to cover seconds or next-day meals. You can combine leftovers with Hearty appetite for big eaters.
Are the costs exact?
No—this is a planning estimate. Subtotal uses rounded raw pounds × price, then adds your tax and fees percentages.
