FAQs

How many cupcakes per person?
Plan about 1 cupcake per adult, about 1.25 for teens, and about 0.75 for children as a dessert. Party main dessert tends to run heavier; wedding mini bites run lighter.
What do the size classes mean?
Mini about 1.0 oz each, Standard about 2.5 oz each, Jumbo about 4.0 oz each. We use this to convert cupcake counts into about cupcake pounds for planning and shipping.
Dessert table, wedding mini bites, or party main dessert — what changes?
Course multipliers: Dessert 1.00x, Wedding mini 0.65x, Party main dessert 1.20x, Coffee break 0.75x. Combine with appetite, event length, and leftovers to fit your crowd.
How are cupcake pounds estimated?
We multiply cupcakes by the per-piece weight for your size class and convert to pounds. Pounds round to the nearest 1/4 lb for planning.
How does pricing work here?
Subtotal = unit price applied to the rounded quantity for your mode: per dozen (rounded dozens × price per dozen) or per each (rounded cupcakes × price per cupcake). Then we add your tax and any extra fees to estimate a total and per-person cost.

Quick notes

  • Base portions (as dessert): Adults about 1; Teens about 1.25; Children about 0.75.
  • Course multipliers: Dessert 1.00x; Wedding mini 0.65x; Party main dessert 1.20x; Coffee 0.75x.
  • Size classes: Mini about 1.0 oz each; Standard about 2.5 oz; Jumbo about 4.0 oz.
  • Context multipliers: Appetite (0.90 / 1.00 / 1.20); Event length (0.95 / 1.00 / 1.08 / 1.15); Leftovers (No / +10% / +20%).
  • Rounding: Cupcakes round up to whole cupcakes; dozens round up to whole dozens for pricing; pounds round to the nearest 1/4 lb.
  • Disclaimer: Estimates only; confirm with your bakery/caterer and adjust for your crowd.
