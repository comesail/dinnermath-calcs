FAQs

How many cupcakes per person?
Plan ~1 standard cupcake or ~2 mini cupcakes per adult-equivalent; adjust for your spread.
What if I serve other desserts?
Use Light appetite or shorter event length to reduce the total, or choose minis to stretch variety further.
Should I round up?
Yes—cupcakes are easy to send home or save for brunch; rounding up prevents shortfalls.
