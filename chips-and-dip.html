Questions and answers about chips and dip per person

How much chips and dip per person?
For light snacks, plan about one and a half ounces of chips and one ounce of dip per adult. For mixed parties, plan about two and a half ounces of chips and one and a half ounces of dip. For heavy game nights, plan about four ounces of chips and two ounces of dip per adult. Teens count as about three quarters of an adult; children count as about one half. Adjust for appetite, event length, and leftovers.
How many bags and cups of dip for 10 people?
With a standard 12 ounce bag and “mixed party” settings, expect roughly 2 bags of chips and about 2 cups of dip. Use the calculator for your exact mix and appetite.
How many bags and cups of dip for 25 people?
About 5 to 6 bags and 4 to 5 cups of dip under “mixed party.” Tap the preset and tweak Adults, Teens, or Children as needed.
How many bags and cups of dip for 50 people?
About 10 to 11 bags and 8 to 9 cups of dip for a mixed party. Heavier game nights will need more.
How many bags and cups of dip for 100 people?
About 20 to 21 bags and 16 to 18 cups of dip in a mixed party setting. Consider whether you want leftovers.
What bag size do you assume?
Default is a 12 ounce bag. You can change to 10, 13, or 15 ounce in the Package sizes row; the bag count will update automatically.
How do I budget total cost?
Enter a price per bag for chips. For dip, choose the pricing mode: price per jar (jar size is selected in Inputs) or homemade (price per ounce). We calculate subtotal, tax and fees, total, and cost per person automatically.

Quick notes

  • Baselines per adult: Light snacks about 1.5 oz chips + 1.0 oz dip; Mixed party about 2.5 oz chips + 1.5 oz dip; Game night heavy about 4.0 oz chips + 2.0 oz dip.
  • Guest weighting: Teens are about 0.75 of an adult; Children are about 0.5 of an adult.
  • Dip style: Thick or loaded adds about ten percent dip; Light or thin reduces about ten percent.
  • Packages: We round chips to whole bags and dip to cups. You can change bag size, dip cup ounces, and dip jar size in Inputs.
  • Context: Appetite, event length, and leftovers can shift totals by ten to twenty percent.
  • Disclaimer: Estimates only; confirm with your store sizes and crowd preferences.
