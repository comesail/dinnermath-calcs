FAQs

How many pounds of Dungeness crab per person?
For a crab main, plan roughly 8–12 ounces per adult (about ½–¾ lb raw). For appetizers, plan 3–5 ounces per adult. Adjust for appetite, event length, and leftovers.
How do I pick a crab weight?
Your market may label average whole-crab weights. Pick the closest option; we use it to convert pounds to approximate whole crabs.
Are results raw or cooked?
Raw purchase weight. Cooked yield depends on prep and moisture loss.
