FAQs

How many doughnuts per person?
Plan ~1.2 ring doughnuts per adult-equivalent (or ~1 filled / ~2 minis), then adjust appetite and event length.
How many dozen should I buy?
Divide the total by 12 and round up. Add a small buffer for late arrivals.
What if I offer multiple flavors?
Add a small buffer so popular flavors last; minis are great for variety.
