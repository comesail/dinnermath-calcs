FAQs

How much rice per person?
As a side, plan about 1 cup cooked per adult, about 1.25 cups for teens, and about 0.75 cup for children. Entree portions are heavier; tasting flights are lighter. Appetite, event length, and leftovers adjust the estimate.
Uncooked vs cooked — how do you convert?
About 1 lb uncooked rice yields 3 lb cooked, which is about 12 cups. We use that to convert cups to uncooked and cooked pounds.
What do the pan sizes mean?
We list pans by dimensions with an about cooked cup capacity: 8x8 inch about 6 cups; 9x13 inch about 12 cups; half steam pan 12x10x2.5 in about 25 cups; full steam pan 20x12x2.5 in about 50 cups.
How does pricing work here?
Subtotal equals the unit price applied to the rounded quantity for your mode: per lb uncooked or per pan. Then tax and any extra fees are added for a total and per-person estimate.
Do you round up?
Pans round up to whole pans; pounds display to one decimal. Add leftovers of plus 10 percent or plus 20 percent for buffer.
