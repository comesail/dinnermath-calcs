FAQs

How many macarons per person?
Plan about 2 standard macarons per adult-equivalent (or ~3 mini / ~1.5 large). Adjust with appetite and event length.
How many boxes should I order?
Take the total macarons and divide by your box size (e.g., 6, 12). Round up for favors and display refills.
What if there are many other desserts?
Use Light appetite or shorten event length to reduce the total.
