FAQs

How much cornbread per person?
As a side, plan about 1 piece per adult, about 1.25 for teens, and about 0.5 for children. BBQ plates or chili nights tend to run heavier.
What do the pan sizes mean?
8x8 in square ~ 9 pieces; 10x10 in square ~ 16; 9x13 in rectangle ~ 20; 12 in skillet ~ 28 wedges; 12x18 in sheet ~ 48. We use these to convert pieces into about how many pans.
How do muffin sizes work?
Mini about 1.0 oz each (about 24 per mini pan); Standard about 2.5 oz each (about 12 per pan); Jumbo about 4.0 oz each (about 6 per pan). We convert needed pieces to about dozens and pounds.
How does pricing work here?
Subtotal = unit price applied to the rounded quantity for your mode: per pan (rounded pans × price per pan), per muffin (rounded muffins × price per muffin), or per dozen muffins (rounded dozens × price per dozen). Then we add your tax and any extra fees to estimate a total and per-person cost.
Do you round up?
Pans round up to whole pans; dozens round up to whole dozens; pounds round to the nearest 1/4 lb for planning. Use leftovers +10% or +20% for extra buffer.
