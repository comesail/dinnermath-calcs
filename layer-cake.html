FAQs

How many slices per round cake?
As a rule of thumb: 8″ ≈ 12, 9″ ≈ 16, 10″ ≈ 20 slices for typical party slices.
What is “wedding-style” slicing?
Thinner rectangular slices that increase yield; pick “Small slice” to scale down per-person needs.
Tier suggestions vs single rounds?
Tiered combos (e.g., 10″+8″) add up yields cleanly and look festive; single-size rounds simplify serving.
Should I add cupcakes or sheet cake?
If you’re short a few slices, cupcakes or a small sheet cake topper fills the gap without buying another full round.
