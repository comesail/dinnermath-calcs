FAQs

How many tenderloins per person?
A whole tenderloin is ~1 lb. That serves about 1.5 adult-equivalents as a main course.
Yield loss?
Tenderloin has very little trim waste, ~95% yield cooked.
Buffet vs plated?
Buffets often stretch further with sides; plated mains stick to the 6 oz cooked baseline.
