FAQs

How many chicken wing drumettes per person?
For a main course, plan about 8 drumettes per adult-equivalent. For a mixed menu use ~6, and for appetizers use ~4 per adult-equivalent. Adjust appetite, length, and leftovers as needed.
How do pieces translate to pounds?
Use the size selector: Small ≈ 12 pieces per pound, Medium ≈ 10, Large ≈ 8. The calculator converts the total pieces to pounds automatically.
Does this account for cooked yield?
Yes indirectly. Portions are based on pieces per adult-equivalent; pounds are estimated from pieces-per-pound averages. Cooked yield (70–75%) is an FYI only and not required for planning pieces.
What if drumettes are part of a big buffet?
Choose Mixed menu or Appetizers to reduce the wings share, or set appetite to “Light.”
Can I estimate trays?
Yes. We show an approximate count of 50-piece trays based on your total pieces. Round up for service convenience.
Averages vary by supplier; confirm piece sizes and counts-per-pound with your market.
