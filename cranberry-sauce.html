Quick notes

  • Base portions (ounces per person): Adults about 2.5 oz; Teens about 2.0 oz; Children about 1.5 oz.
  • Homemade options: 2.0 oz (about 1/4 cup), 3.0 oz (about 3/8 cup), 4.0 oz (about 1/2 cup) per person.
  • Premade containers: 8 oz jar, 14 oz can, 16 oz jar, 24 oz jar, 32 oz tub/jar.
  • Course multipliers: Side 1.00x; Mixed 0.75x; Heavy 1.25x; Appetizer 0.50x.
  • Context multipliers: Appetite (0.90 / 1.00 / 1.20); Event length (0.95 / 1.00 / 1.08 / 1.15); Leftovers (No / +10% / +20%).
  • Rounding: Cups and quarts round to the nearest 1/4; Raw pounds round to the nearest 1/4 lb. Containers round up to whole containers.
  • Disclaimer: Estimates only; confirm with your grocer/caterer and adjust for your crowd.
