FAQs

How many cups per person?
Plan ~½ cup (4 fl oz) per adult-equivalent. Use Tasting or Generous to adjust.
How many ramekins do I need?
We divide total ounces by the container size you select and round up.
Does mousse need more?
Mousse feels lighter; some crowds may want a bit more—use Generous or add a buffer.
